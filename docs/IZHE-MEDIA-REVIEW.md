# IZHE Source Media Review

This review covers every unique asset originally uploaded to `IZHE Resource Folder`. Four byte-identical `- Copy` files were removed. High-resolution originals are preserved under `IZHE Resource Folder/originals/`; optimized WebP derivatives are published under `public/assets/media/izhe/`.

## Governance conclusion

Eleven no-person IZHE brand, teaching, mockup, and flat-lay assets are now automatically processed as **Approved for general website/editorial use**. These records are marked `rightsStatus: owned_no_people` and appear enabled in the visual editor and structured-content media pickers.

Recognizable-person photographs are not auto-approved until a model/photo release is recorded. Apparel concepts and legacy flat lays may be used in general website sections, but they are not approved as current catalog product images until matched to an active SKU, color, garment, print placement, and fulfillment capability.

## Approval workflow

1. No-person source assets identified above are processed automatically for general site/editorial use.
2. Obtain and retain a release for every recognizable person before changing `rightsStatus` to `release_on_file`.
3. Match apparel imagery to an active SKU before changing `productAccuracyStatus` to `accurate` or assigning it to a current product listing.
4. Manual administrator review overrides the automatic source policy.
5. Use the structured-content or visual-editor picker to place approved assets.
