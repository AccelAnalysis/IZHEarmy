BOOK ONE · PREVIEW

Who Is God to You?

Discovering God Through His Names

The God Who Is

Before Scripture tells us what God does, it reveals who He is. When Moses asked what name he should carry back to Israel, God answered from His own eternal being: “I AM WHO I AM.”

IZHE begins there. Before asking, “Is He your Provider?” or “Is He your Healer?” we begin with the deeper question: Do you know the One who is present, self-existent, faithful, and unchanged?

Each chapter pairs a biblical name or title with Scripture, reflection, and a simple invitation to conversation. The aim is not merely to learn a list of names, but to recognize the character of God and answer personally: Who is He to you?

The book explains the message. The shirt gives someone a reason to ask about it.
